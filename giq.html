5 Core Pillars

YOUR IQ SCORED ACROSS EVERY DIMENSION.

Every question maps to one of five pillars. Your score shows exactly where your football knowledge is strong — and where to build.

01
Rules & Objectives
Scoring, downs, penalties, turnovers, and how the game is officiated from kickoff to final whistle.
02
Positions & Responsibilities
Every position on offense, defense, and special teams — what they do, why they do it, and how they fit the play.
03
Formations & Alignments
How offenses and defenses line up, why spacing matters, and what pre-snap alignment reveals about the play.
04
Plays & Concepts
Run and pass concepts, coverage schemes, blitz packages, route combinations, and how plays are designed to attack defenses.
05
Situational Awareness
3rd down decisions, red zone strategy, clock management, 4th down calls, and reading the game situation in real time.
