The Core Concept

GRIDIRON IQ IS THE EDGE MOST PLAYERS DON’T HAVE.

Most players know their assignment. The ones who stand out understand the entire play — what the defense is showing, why the blocking scheme works, and how to react when the play breaks down.

That’s Gridiron IQ. It isn’t about size or speed. It’s about football intelligence — and it can be developed. Players with a high Gridiron IQ process the game faster, adjust when things break down, and get more out of every rep at practice.

First Down Academy builds it systematically. Every slide. Every module. Every quiz — designed to raise football IQ so players arrive at practice with the foundation already installed.

01
GAME STRUCTURE
Field layout, downs, scoring, penalties, and how a football game works from kickoff to final whistle.
