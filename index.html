Why Gridiron IQ Matters

FOOTBALL IQ IS THE EDGE MOST PLAYERS DON’T HAVE.

Most players know their assignment. The ones who stand out understand the entire play — what the defense is showing, why the blocking scheme works, and how to react when everything breaks down.

That’s Gridiron IQ. It isn’t about size or speed. It’s about football intelligence — and it can be built systematically. Players who understand the whole game process decisions faster, adjust when plays break down, and get more out of every rep at practice.

First Down Academy measures it, shows you where to improve, and helps you build it over time.
